# Department: Family

## Purpose
Defines how MiM Brain handles personal and family-related communications that arrive in the CEO's work inbox.

## CEO Role
Handled directly by CEO. Occasionally routes to internal teams when personal contacts surface business opportunities.

## Routing Rules

| Topic | Owner |
|-------|-------|
| Hiring referrals | Recruiting |
| Partnership intros | Partnerships |
| Sales leads | Sales |
| Media opportunities | Marketing |
| Everything else | CEO directly |

## Importance Model

### High
- High-value introductions
- Strategic opportunities from personal network
- Key personal relationships
- Kids sports deadlines (registration, game changes)

### Medium
- Networking conversations
- Professional advice requests
- Routine kids sports communications

### Low
- Casual catch-ups
- Event invites
- General networking outreach
