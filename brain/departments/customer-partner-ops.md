# Department: Customer / Partner Ops

## Purpose
Defines how In Motion prioritizes customer and partner operations activity.

## Triage Hierarchy

```
Customer risk
  ↓
Revenue impact
  ↓
Operational blockers
  ↓
Implementation progress
  ↓
Informational updates
```

## CEO Routing Rules

The CEO rarely handles execution — only ownership assignment.

| Email Type | Forward To |
|------------|-----------|
| Sales opportunity | Sales lead |
| Customer issue or escalation | Customer success / support |
| Product feedback | Head of product |
| Technical integration question | Engineering lead |
| Pricing negotiation | Sales / revenue lead |
| Integration partner | Product or engineering |
| Distribution partnership | Head of partnerships / BD |
| Marketing collaboration | Head of marketing |
| Strategic alliance | BD / COO |

In early startups (≤10 people), most things route to just three people: head of sales, head of product, operations / chief of staff.

## Importance Model

### Critical (same day or hours)

| Situation | Signals | Why |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| Customer escalation | Escalation, urgent issue, outage, blocking, production incident | Risk of churn, revenue loss, brand damage |
| Revenue / deal impact | Renewal risk, expansion, enterprise request, deal blocker | Direct revenue impact |
| Strategic partner request | Joint launch, partnership rollout, exec intro | Impacts growth channels |

### Medium (within a day or two)

| Situation | Signals | Why |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| Implementation & onboarding | Timeline, deployment, setup | Important for success, not urgent |
| Feedback & feature requests | Feature request, product feedback | Valuable input, rarely urgent |
| Partner coordination | Partner update, joint marketing, integration planning | Keeps relationships moving |

### Low (informational)

| Situation | Signals | Why |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| Status updates | FYI, update, summary, notes | No action required |
| Scheduling | Calendar, meeting, availability | Operational detail |
| Announcements | Newsletter, general update | No action required |
