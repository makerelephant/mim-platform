# Department: Administration

## Purpose
Defines how MiM Brain prioritizes and routes administrative and operational activity. Includes system/account notifications, HR, office, vendor management, and internal ops.

## Routing Rules

| Topic | Owner |
|-------|-------|
| Office operations | Operations |
| HR issues | People ops |
| Travel planning | Executive assistant |
| Legal paperwork | Legal / ops |
| Vendor management | Operations |

## Importance Model

### High
- HR incidents
- Legal compliance tasks
- Operational emergencies
- Executive scheduling conflicts
- Security alerts

### Medium
- Hiring coordination
- Travel planning
- Vendor management

### Low
- Office logistics
- Administrative reminders
- Internal announcements
