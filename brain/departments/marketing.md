# Department: Marketing

## Purpose
Defines how In Motion prioritizes and routes marketing activity. Includes industry research, competitive intelligence, market development, and relationships & PR.

## Routing Rules

| Topic | Owner |
|-------|-------|
| Marketing campaigns | Head of marketing |
| Content / blog | Marketing team |
| PR requests | PR or marketing |
| Partnerships / co-marketing | Partnerships / marketing |
| Website updates | Marketing / design |
| Lead generation | Growth / marketing |

## Importance Model

### High
- Major marketing campaign launches
- PR interviews or media opportunities
- Strategic partnerships tied to marketing
- Messaging or brand positioning decisions

### Medium
- Campaign reviews
- Content approvals
- Website updates
- Event participation

### Low
- Vendor pitches
- Marketing newsletters
- Promotional outreach
- Tool demos
