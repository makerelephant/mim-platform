# Department: AI

## Purpose
Defines how MiM Brain prioritizes and routes AI-related activity. AI is enabling every corner of the business and is a prominent part of every day.

## Routing Rules

| Topic | Owner |
|-------|-------|
| Model development | AI / ML engineering |
| AI product features | Product + AI team |
| AI infrastructure | Engineering |
| AI vendor tools | Engineering / product |
| Research collaboration | AI leadership |

## Importance Model

### High
- Core AI product features
- Model performance issues
- Ethical / safety concerns
- Major AI capability improvements

### Medium
- Model experiments
- AI feature ideas
- Research discussions

### Low
- AI tool sales pitches
- AI newsletters
- Vendor product updates
