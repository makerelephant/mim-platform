# Email Classification Rule: Administration

## Category ID
`administration`

## Inbound From
- Operations team
- Executive assistant
- HR / people ops
- Office management
- Vendors
- System notifications (Google Voice, Slack admin, LinkedIn, security alerts)
- SaaS platforms (account notifications, access alerts)

## Outbound To
- Operations
- People ops / HR
- Executive assistant
- Legal / ops (paperwork)
- Operations (vendor management)

## Signals

### HR / People
HR, people ops, employee, hiring, onboarding, offboarding, benefits, PTO, leave, employee handbook, performance review

### Office / Facilities
Office, lease, sublease, facilities, building, parking, access card, mail, supplies, move-in

### System / Account
Password, account, login, verification, two-factor, 2FA, API key, access, permissions, notification settings, system alert, admin update

### Vendor Management
Vendor, subscription, renewal, license, SaaS, tool, platform, service agreement

### Travel
Travel, flight, hotel, booking, itinerary, travel policy, expense

### Internal Ops
Operations, process, workflow, internal announcement, company update, all-hands, team update

## Importance

### High
- HR incidents
- Legal compliance tasks
- Operational emergencies
- Executive scheduling conflicts
- Security alerts (unauthorized access, credential issues)

### Medium
- Hiring coordination
- Travel planning
- Vendor management
- Office logistics requiring decisions

### Low
- Office logistics (routine)
- Administrative reminders
- Internal announcements
- System notifications (routine)
- Subscription renewal reminders
