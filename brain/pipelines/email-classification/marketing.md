# Email Classification Rule: Marketing

## Category ID
`marketing`

## Includes
Industry research, competitive intelligence, market development, relationships & PR

## Inbound From
- Head of marketing / growth team
- PR agencies
- Journalists / media
- Partners requesting marketing collaboration
- Sales team (campaign alignment)
- Event organizers
- Industry newsletters and research sources
- Competitors (indirectly, via market signals)

## Outbound To
- Head of marketing
- Marketing team (content, blog)
- PR / communications
- Partnerships / marketing (co-marketing)
- Marketing / design (website)
- Growth / marketing (lead generation)

## Signals

### Campaigns & Content
Campaign, content marketing, blog, newsletter, social media, launch campaign, content calendar, editorial, content strategy, SEO, lead generation, growth

### PR & Media
Press, media, interview, journalist, press release, media opportunity, PR, coverage, editorial, podcast, quote request

### Brand & Positioning
Brand positioning, messaging, value proposition, brand story, positioning statement, competitive positioning

### Industry Research
Industry research, market report, market analysis, trend, industry trend, market data, research paper, whitepaper

### Competitive Intelligence
Competitor, competitive analysis, market share, competitive landscape, competitor launch, competitive positioning

### Market Development
Market development, market expansion, new market, market opportunity, go-to-market, GTM, market entry

### Relationships & PR
Introduction, networking, strategic relationship, partnership, co-marketing, joint marketing, event, conference, speaking opportunity

## Importance

### High
- Major marketing campaign launches
- PR interviews or media opportunities
- Strategic partnerships tied to marketing
- Messaging or brand positioning decisions
- Competitive intelligence requiring immediate response

### Medium
- Campaign reviews
- Content approvals
- Website updates
- Event participation
- Industry research reports
- Market development discussions

### Low
- Vendor pitches
- Marketing newsletters
- Promotional outreach
- Tool demos
- General industry reading
