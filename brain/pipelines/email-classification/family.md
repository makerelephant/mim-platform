# Email Classification Rule: Family

## Category ID
`family`

## Inbound From
- Friends or personal contacts
- Former colleagues
- Networking contacts
- Mentors or advisors
- Community members
- Kids sports organizations (LeagueApps, teams, coaches)
- Family members
- School communications

## Outbound To
- Often handled directly by CEO
- Recruiting (hiring referrals)
- Partnerships (partnership intros)
- Sales (sales leads)
- Marketing (media opportunities)

## Signals

### Personal Relationships
Personal, family, friend, former colleague, mentor, catch up, coffee, reunion, congratulations, birthday, holiday

### Kids Sports
Hockey, tournament, registration, roster, practice, game schedule, team, coach, LeagueApps, league, player, tryout, uniform

### Community
Community, volunteer, charity, nonprofit, donation, fundraiser, board member, advisory

### Networking (Personal)
Introduction, referral, recommendation, "thought of you", "you should meet", alumni, reunion

## Importance

### High
- High-value introductions
- Strategic opportunities from personal network
- Key personal relationships
- Kids sports logistics requiring immediate action (registration deadlines, game changes)

### Medium
- Networking conversations
- Professional advice requests
- Kids sports routine communications

### Low
- Casual catch-ups
- Event invites
- General networking outreach
- Community newsletters
