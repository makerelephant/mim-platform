# Email Classification Rule: Customer / Partner Ops

## Category ID
`customer_partner_ops`

## Inbound From
- Key customers
- Prospective enterprise customers
- Support escalations
- Customer success managers
- Potential partners
- Integration / platform partners
- Channel partners, resellers
- Investors introducing partnerships

## Outbound To
- Sales lead
- Customer success / support
- Head of product
- Engineering lead
- Head of partnerships / BD
- Head of marketing
- Operations / COO
- Finance / legal counsel

## Signals

### Customer Relationship
Customer, client, account, user, deployment, onboarding, adoption, renewal, "customer feedback", "customer request", "customer issue", "account update", "renewal discussion"

### Support & Issues
Issue, bug, support, ticket, incident, escalation, troubleshooting, "customer reported issue", "support escalation", "investigating bug", "follow-up on ticket"

### Product Usage / Integration
Integration, API, implementation, setup, configuration, deployment, "integration support", "API access request", "implementation timeline"

### Partner Operations
Partner, integration partner, channel, reseller, vendor, platform, "partner onboarding", "partner enablement", "partner integration", "joint customer opportunity"

### Project Coordination
Timeline, milestone, deliverables, rollout, launch, "next steps", "action items", "project update", "implementation plan"

### Collaboration Language
"Looping in", "following up", "next steps", "action items", "sharing an update", "let's coordinate", "please advise"

## Importance

### High
- Customer escalation: urgent issue, service outage, blocking issue, production incident, major bug
- Revenue or deal impact: renewal risk, expansion opportunity, enterprise customer request, deal blocker, contract timing
- Strategic partner request: joint launch, partnership rollout, executive introduction, key partner coordination

### Medium
- Customer implementation and onboarding
- Customer feedback and feature requests
- Partner coordination and joint marketing
- Integration planning

### Low
- Status updates and FYI emails
- Scheduling and logistics for partner/customer calls
- Partner newsletters and informational announcements
