# Email Classification Rule: Fundraising

## Category ID
`fundraising`

## Inbound From
- Investors (potential and existing)
- Advisors
- Legal counsel
- Professional network (referrals, warm intros)

## Outbound To
- Investors (potential and existing)
- Advisors
- Legal counsel
- Internal founders
- Professional network

## Signals
Term sheet, SAFE, convertible note, cap table, valuation, due diligence, pitch deck, investor update, data room, round, pre-seed, seed, Series A/B/C, commitment, syndication, closing, lead investor, one pager, angel, oversubscribed, pipeline, traction metrics

## Importance

### High
- Term sheet received or discussed
- Due diligence request
- Closing mechanics
- Commitment or verbal commit
- Lead investor communication
- Valuation discussion
- Negotiation on terms

### Medium
- Active investor conversation thread
- Meeting follow-up
- Deck or one-pager request
- Warm introduction to investor
- Investor update sent or received
- Financial model discussion
- Data room access

### Low
- Cold inbound from unknown investor
- General networking with fundraising mention
- Scheduling logistics for investor meeting
- Demo day mention
- Newsletter or content about fundraising trends
